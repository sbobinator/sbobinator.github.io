03. Privacy

Your Files Stay Yours

Cloud transcription means sending voice recordings to someone else's servers. Sbobinator runs on your computer — so client calls, medical notes, legal interviews, and personal content never need to leave your desk.

No upload for transcription

The speech engine runs locally. After a one-time model download, you can transcribe without an internet connection.

Install on your own PC

Windows for everyday use, or a small home server if you want it always available on your network.

AI summaries only if you switch them on

Need a short recap? Enable summaries and pick your AI provider. Otherwise you get text and subtitles — nothing else.
